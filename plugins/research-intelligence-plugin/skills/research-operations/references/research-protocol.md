# Research Protocol

Use this staged protocol for comprehensive research.

## 1. Initial Exploration

- Perform broad discovery to define topic boundaries.
- Identify core sub-topics, terminology, and key concepts.

## 2. In-Depth Investigation

- Gather detailed information for each sub-topic.
- Include differing viewpoints, theories, arguments, and counterarguments.

## 3. Critical Analysis

- Identify patterns, relationships, contradictions, and consensus areas.
- Mark confidence and unresolved ambiguity for each major claim.

## 4. Knowledge Synthesis

- Build an integrated understanding across all sub-topics.
- Distinguish established facts from contested interpretations.

## 5. Context and Trajectory

- Summarize historical background and current state.
- Assess likely future developments and leading indicators.

## 6. Key Entities

- List influential people, seminal works, major organizations, and major events.

## Output Contract

Return:

- `summary`: concise synthesis of the topic.
- `key_entities`: people, works, organizations, events.
- `evidence_table`: claims with source references and confidence.
- `open_questions`: unresolved issues and what evidence would resolve them.
