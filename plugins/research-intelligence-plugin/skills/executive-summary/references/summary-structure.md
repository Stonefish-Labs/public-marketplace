# Executive Summary Structure Contract

## Section Requirements

1. Situation overview (50-75 words)
- What is happening now and why it matters.
- Current vs desired state gap.

2. Key findings (125-175 words)
- 3-5 critical insights.
- Each insight includes at least one quantitative metric.
- Prioritize by business impact.

3. Business impact (50-75 words)
- Revenue/cost/market implications.
- Risk or opportunity scale with probability when possible.
- Time horizon for impact realization.

4. Recommendations (75-100 words)
- 3-4 prioritized actions.
- Include owner, timeline, expected outcome.

5. Next steps (25-50 words)
- 2-3 immediate actions within 30 days.
- Include decision point and deadline.

## Final Constraints

- Target: 325-475 words.
- Hard maximum: 500 words.
- Direct language; avoid filler and hedging.
