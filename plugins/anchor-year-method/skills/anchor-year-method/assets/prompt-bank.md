# Prompt Bank

Use these prompts when the user needs help recalling details.

## Choose An Anchor Year

- Which year feels emotionally charged or memorable right now?
- If no single year stands out, pick a school year, job year, or moving year.

## Rebuild World Context

- What songs or artists were everywhere that year?
- What movies or shows did people talk about constantly?
- What technology felt new or exciting then?
- What styles or trends do you remember seeing often?

## Rebuild Personal Snapshot

- How old were you, and where were you living?
- What was your school or work situation?
- Who were your closest people at the time?
- What did a normal weekday look like?

## Passions And Events

- What hobby or interest took most of your attention?
- Which holidays or birthdays from that year stand out?
- Were there any milestones or turning points?

## Memory Chain Follow-Ups

- What happened right before that moment?
- What happened next?
- Who else was involved?
- What object, smell, sound, or place do you connect with it?
- What emotion was strongest in that memory?

## Gentle Redirects

- We can switch to lighter memories if that feels better.
- Want to focus on music, hobbies, or holiday moments first?
- We can capture bullet points now and flesh out full stories later.
